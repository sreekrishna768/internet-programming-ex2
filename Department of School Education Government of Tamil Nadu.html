Studio at School

This Govt HSS school at Othakalmandapam has a wonderful studio set up  for taking online classes. With a consistent generation of content they have a strong presence in social media. Several school students are active subscribers of the school’s YouTube channel. The initiative is driven by the school’s HM and a few dedicated teachers such as Mr.Nagaraj who is a teacher with visual impairment.
